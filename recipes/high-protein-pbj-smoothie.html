Ingredients

  • Frozen fruit of choice
    • Blueberries
    • Raspberries
    • Mixed Berry Blend
  • Bananas
  • Dates
  • Agave
  • Peanut Butter or Almond Butter
  • Greek Yogurt

Steps to Create Beautiful PBJ Smoothies

  1. Get out the blender
  2. Add frozen fruit of choice (1/2 cup)
  3. Add one banana
  4. Add two dates
  5. Add sweetner as desired
  6. Add 3 tbsp nut butter of choice
  7. Add 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  8. Add 1 cup water
  9. Blend and serve!
