Ingredients

  • Red lentil pasta (I like banza)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Cherry or roma tomatoes

Steps to Create Yummy Penne Pasta

  1. Prepare pasta according to package instructions
  2. Saute tomatoes in pan until they become soft and juicy
  3. Assemble pasta, top with sauce and parmesan, add basil for garnish
