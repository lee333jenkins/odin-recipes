Ingredients

  • rice
  • salmon
  • cucumber
  • carrots (shredded)
  • avocado
  • soy sauce or coconut aminos
  • low-fat avocado mayo (optional)

Steps to Create Delicious Salmon Bowls

  1. Prepare your rice
  2. Bake salmon, check out our easy air fryer salmon recipe!
  3. Prepare toppings:
    • Slice avocado
    • Prepare edamame according to package instructions
    • Shred carrots (I like to use a food processor for easy shredding!)
    • Slice cucumber
  4. Additional Toppings (Optional):
    • Kimchi (choose a mild option for less spice)
    • Seaweed, great for GERD due to alginates
    • Steamed spinach or other leafy green
  5. Assemble bowls
